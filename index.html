The format of this bracket is...

You are given a pick on each series in the round. If you guess the winner right, you get 2 points, and if you correctly guess the winner and number of games then you get 5 points.

Each pick here will return 2 for the correct team and 5 for the correct team and number of games.

In the second, third and final round you will be able to bet the points you've accrued from previous rounds to the current round, with the same returns (2 for winner, 5 for winner and team)... but if you don't get the winner correct, you will lose those points.

You need to use the 10 extra picks provided, but do not need to bet any of your own points from previous rounds.
