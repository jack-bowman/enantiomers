Welcome!

My name is Jack Bowman, thank you for visiting my website!

I am a recent (2023) graduate of Western University, where I obtained a BSc Honours Specialization in Chemistry. I have a passion for all things chemistry, I love organic synthesis, materials chemistry and polymer work, quantum chemistry, you name it. Most recently, my experience has been in the Workentin group at Western, towards the development of silver and gold nanoclusters with surface functionalities towards a variety of applications. Check out the group here! I have also spent some time with the Gobbo group at the University of Trieste, working on biomimetic materials in the field of bottom-up synthetic biology.

Throughout my undergraduate degree, I have received 3 NSERC Undergraduate Student Research Awards (USRAs), awarded on the basis of academic standing, as well as a MITACS Globalink Award allowing me to carry out research in Trieste, Italy. I have also received the James D. McNabb Award at Western University, awarded to the student registered in fourth year of an Honors Specialization in Chemistry with he best overall record in the third year of the program, as well as a UWO In-Course Scholarship Year IV, awarded on academic standing.

As of Septmeber 2023, I have begun a MSc as an NSERC CGS-M recipient at Western University, studying as a joint student between the Workentin group and Gilroy group. I am working on joining polymers and nanoclusters through click chemistry. I look forward to updating you on it!

Outside of chemistry, I love to play sports, with a non-exhaustive list including hockey, baseball, tennis, and pickleball. I also enjoy a hike, a swim at the beach, a free skate, and other activities (I am amenable).

Beyond the outdoors, I love chess and am a sore loser and very graceful winner, I enjoy coding to automate my life's tasks and write in python, as well as html, javascript and css. Maybe our music tastes overlap, you can see what I listen to here if you promise not to judge.

Please reach out to me if you have any questions or oppotunities, you can find information on how to reach me below!
