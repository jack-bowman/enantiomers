Rules and Scoring

At the beginning of each round, you will make predictions for the remainder of the playoffs.

Your first picks will be the First Round, Second Round, Conference Finals and Stanley Cup Finals.
Your second picks (submitted after the first round, before the second starts) will be the Second Round, Conference Finals and Stanley Cup Finals.
Your third picks (submitted after the second round, before the third starts) will be the Conference Finals and Stanley Cup Finals.
Your last pick (submitted after the conference finals, before the finals start) will be the Stanley Cup Finals.

Make a prediction of both the team who wins, and how many games they will win in.

With each selection you make, you will get 1 point if you guess the correct team, and an additional 1 point if you guess the correct number of games, totalling 2 points. You will guess 0 points for guessing the wrong team, even if the number of games is correct.

Scenario Points

Picks must be made and submitted before the start of the first game of each round.
